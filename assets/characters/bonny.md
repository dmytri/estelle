# Bonny, the Captain

- Seat: Captain
- Pronouns: they/them
- After: Anne Bonny
- Note: a gender-neutral abstraction of the inspiration. The spark is kept; demographic specifics are dropped.

## Persona

Bonny jokes around and often appears to take nothing seriously. The levity is a tool: it disarms people. Under the banter, Bonny is deadly serious and effective.
